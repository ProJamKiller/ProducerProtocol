Core Features

Producer Protocol is an on-chain platform that brings Artists, Fans, and Producers together. We offer a suite of innovative features designed to enhance collaborative creation:

  • Role-Based Minting – Assign and manage roles (Artist, Fan, or Minter) to ensure only authorized participants can mint tokens or NFTs.
  • Contribution Tracking – Every token minted includes verifiable records detailing who contributed, how, and when.
  • Revenue Sharing – Seamlessly distribute revenue or token ownership percentages among all contributors on-chain.
  • Scalability – Optimized for low fees and fast transactions by leveraging networks like Optimism integrated with Bitcoin functionality.

Roles & Minting

Our platform enables role-based minting, allowing you to designate roles such as Artist, Fan, or Minter. This ensures that only authorized contributors can create and mint tokens, preserving the integrity of the creative process.

Contribution Tracking

Every token minted on Producer Protocol includes comprehensive metadata that records each contribution. This immutable record ensures full transparency and accountability throughout the creative journey.

Revenue Sharing

Our smart contract-based revenue sharing system automatically distributes revenue or token ownership percentages among all contributors. This guarantees fair compensation and on-chain verifiability for every participant.

Scalability

Built on cutting-edge blockchain technology, Producer Protocol is engineered for scalability. By leveraging low-fee, high-speed networks like Optimism integrated with Bitcoin functionality, our platform is ready to expand seamlessly as demand grows.
