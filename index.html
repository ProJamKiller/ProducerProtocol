Producer Protocol

Producer Protocol is an on-chain platform that brings Artists, Fans, and Producers together. We provide role-based minting, contribution tracking, and transparent revenue sharing. Whether you're creating new music, podcasts, or any collaborative media, you can reward your backers and fans while keeping verifiable records on the blockchain.

Core Features:

  • Role-Based Minting: Assign roles like Artist, Fan, or Minter and control who can mint tokens or NFTs.
  • Contribution Tracking: Every token minted includes a record of who contributed, how they contributed, and when.
  • Revenue Sharing: Distribute token ownership or revenue percentages seamlessly between participants.
  • Scalability: Designed to operate across multiple chains (like Ethereum and Optimism) to keep fees low and transactions fast.
